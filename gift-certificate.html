Online Gift Certificates

  • Gift certificates purchased online can be emailed directly to the recipient or printed from your computer.
  • Before a certificate purchased online can be used, the recipient MUST VISIT THE SALON OFFICE TO REGISTER THE CERTIFICATE.
  • Please contact the professionals directly to book your appointment using the phone numbers on our Directory page. Please let the provider know that you will be using a Sedora Salons Gift Certificate.
  • Please present the gift certificate to the provider upon arrival to your appointment.
  • Gift certificates are not redeemable for cash.
  • If the value of the service provided exceeds the value of the gift certificate, please pay the difference directly to the provider.
  • Gratuities are appreciated.
  • A certificate can only be used with one provider. Certificates may be split at the salon office at on charge into smaller amounts to be used with multiple providers.
  • If you must cancel your appointment, please do so by contacting the provider at least 24 hours prior to your scheduled time. Thank you for respecting the value of your scheduled time.
